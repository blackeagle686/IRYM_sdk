# SVU University Insights

The Syrian Virtual University (SVU) is a public university headquartered in Damascus, Syria. 
It was established in 2002 and is the first virtual university in the region.

## Programs
- Information Technology (BAIT)
- Business Administration (BSCE)
- Master in Quality (MIQ)
- Master in Web Technology (MWT)

## Mission
To provide world-class education to students in the region through the latest technology.
